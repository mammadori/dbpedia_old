The '''Ansbach''' was a [[Germany|German]] [[automobile]] manufactured from 1910 to 1913 by the forerunner of [[Faun GmbH|Faun]], a company well known for its trucks and buses.  The 1559 [[cubic centimetre|cc]] [[four-cylinder]] 14 [[horsepower|hp]] Ansbach [[touring car]] was known as the Kautz.
