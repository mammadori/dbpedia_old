{{TOC right}}The '''Suction Diesel Injection''' engine, abbreviated to '''SDI'''<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sgpetch.co.uk/pages/motability/abbreviations|title=Abbreviations|work=SG Petch Ltd|publisher=SGPetch.co.uk|accessdate=4 November 2009}}</ref> is a design of [[naturally aspirated]] [[diesel engine]] developed and produced by the [[Volkswagen Group]] for use in [[automobile|cars]] and [[van]]s, along with [[marine engine|Marine Motor]]<ref name=VW_Marine>{{cite web|url=http://www.vw-m.de/index.php?id=6&L=1|title=Volkswagen Marine > Engines <|work=[[Volkswagen Group]]|publisher=Volkswagen.de|year=2009|accessdate=4 November 2009}}</ref> and Industrial Motor<ref>{{citation|url=http://www.mi-uk.com/products/engines/downloads/sdi19industrial.pdf|title=The SDI 1.9 Industrial Engine|format=PDF|work=[[Volkswagen Group|Volkswagen AG]]|publisher=mi-uk.com|date=March 2005|accessdate=4 November 2009}}</ref> applications.  These engines are produced in various sizes (from 1.7 to 2.5&nbsp;[[litre]]s) and states of tune, depending on application.

The SDI engine is used in applications where [[reliability engineering|reliability]] and [[fuel economy in automobiles|fuel economy]] are of primary concern.  Because these engines lack any form of [[forced induction]], their power output is low compared to a similar [[turbocharged]] engine.  For example, the 2.0 SDI engine fitted to the [[Volkswagen Golf Mk5]] has a peak power output of {{convert|55|kW|PS bhp|0|lk=on}}; whereas the same engine in turbocharged [[Turbocharged Direct Injection]] (TDI) form is rated at {{convert|103|kW|PS bhp|0}} or {{convert|125|kW|PS bhp|0}}, depending on specification.  However, performance at very low engine speeds and when setting off from stationary is often superior to forced induction engines due to the lack of [[turbo lag]].

The 1.9 SDI engine, fitted to the first generation [[Škoda Octavia]], is particularly popular amongst taxi drivers due to its low fuel consumption and ability to cover large mileages (over {{convert|250000|mi}}) without requiring major mechanical attention.{{citation needed|date=November 2009}}
