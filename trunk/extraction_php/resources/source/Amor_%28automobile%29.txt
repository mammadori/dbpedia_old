The '''Amor''' was a small [[Germany|German]] [[automobile]] built in limited numbers in [[Cologne]] from 1924 to 1925; the car had a 16[[Horsepower|hp]] [[four-cylinder]] proprietary engine. Its name means "love" in Spanish and Portuguese.
