The '''BMW M106''' motor replaced the [[BMW M102]] motor. The addition of [[Robert Bosch GmbH|Bosch]] Motronic engine management and an increased displacement to 3.4 liters produced the same output power as the M102 at lower RPM and with better [[Fuel economy in automobiles|fuel economy]], and at only 6 psi vs. the M102 boost pressure of 10 psi. The M106 motor was used to power the 745i E23 until its end of production in 1986.
