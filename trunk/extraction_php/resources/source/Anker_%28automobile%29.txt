The '''Anker''' was a [[Germany|German]] [[automobile]] manufactured in [[Berlin]] between 1919 and 1920.  Cars were built up from war-surplus components of the 1145[[Cubic centimetre|cc]] [[four-cylinder]] [[Wanderer (car)|Wanderer]].
