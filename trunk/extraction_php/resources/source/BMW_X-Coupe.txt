The X-Coupe was a concept car from [[BMW]], debuting at the 2001 [[North American International Auto Show]] in [[Detroit, Michigan|Detroit]]. Based upon the [[BMW X5]] [[chassis]]<ref>[http://www.ultimatecarpage.com/car/738/BMW-X-Coupe.html  "Ultimate Car Page"]</ref>, the X-Coupe featured an [[aluminium]] body and a 3.0 [[litre]] [[turbocharger|turbo]]-[[diesel engine]].
