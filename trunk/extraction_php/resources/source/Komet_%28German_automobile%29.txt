The '''Komet''' was a German [[automobile]] manufactured from 1922 until 1924 by Komet Autofabrik Buchmann & Co of [[Leisnig]].  It was later made by another company as the [[Kenter]], it had a 1060&nbsp;cc [[Steudel]] engine.
