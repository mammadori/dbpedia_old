The '''Volkswagen Group C platform''' is a series of extended [[mid-size car|midsize]] [[automobile platform]]s from the [[Germany|German]] conglomerate [[Volkswagen Group]].  It has been used for luxury automobiles under the [[Audi]] [[marque]].  It is the Group's oldest platform, having started with the [[Audi 100]] in 1969.
