The '''Mercedes-Benz S400 BlueHYBRID ''' is a [[concept car]] [[DaimlerChrysler]] produced under the '''[[Mercedes-Benz]]''' [[brand]]. It débuted at the 2005 [[North American International Auto Show]]. DaimlerChrysler claims the car can accelerate from 0&ndash;100&nbsp;[[kilometre per hour|km/h]] in 7.6&nbsp;s. Furthermore, it claims the car can travel {{convert|34|mpgus|lk=off }} of diesel.
[[Image:NAIAS-2005.jpg|thumbnail|150px|right|The S400 Hybrid (not pictured) débuted at NAIAS 2005.]]
The Hybrid's drivetrain includes a [[V8]] CDI [[internal combustion engine]] and two [[electric motor]]s. The [[diesel engine]] produces 191&nbsp;kW (256.1&nbsp;hp) [[Power (physics)|power]]. The two electric motors together produce an additional 50&nbsp;kW (67&nbsp;hp) for a total of 241&nbsp;kW (323.2&nbsp;hp). A 1.9 [[kilowatt-hour|kWh]] [[nickel metal hydride|NiMH battery]] in the trunk powers the electric motors.
