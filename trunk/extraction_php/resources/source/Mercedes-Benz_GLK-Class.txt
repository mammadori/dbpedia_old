The '''Mercedes-Benz GLK''' (code name X204) is a [[compact SUV|compact]] [[luxury vehicles|luxury]] [[crossover (automobile)|crossover]] released as a 2009 model elsewhere{{Citation needed|date=March 2009}} and as a 2010 model in [[North America]]{{Citation needed|date=March 2009}} after its public debut at the 2008 [[Beijing Auto Show]] alongside the competing [[Audi Q5]].

The GLK-Class was launched in 2008 and is built in Bremen (Germany), the first Mercedes SUV to be built there. [http://www.emercedesbenz.com/Jul08/15_001258_The_Mercedes_Benz_GLK_Class_Design.html Its design] is heavily influenced by the [[Mercedes-Benz C-Class|C-Class]].

On 12 January 2008, actress [[Kim Cattrall]] at a special unveiling ceremony at the [[North American International Auto Show]] in [[Detroit]], confirming that her ''[[Sex and the City]]'' character ([[Samantha Jones]]) would drive the SUV in the movie version of the popular TV show.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.emercedesbenz.com/Apr08/25_001121_Behind_The_Scenes_With_Mercedes_Benz_On_The_Set_Of_Sex_And_The_City.html |title=Behind The Scenes With Mercedes-Benz On The Set Of Sex And The City &#124; eMercedesBenz - The Unofficial Mercedes-Benz Weblog |publisher=eMercedesBenz |date=2008-04-25 |accessdate=2009-10-17}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.emercedesbenz.com/Jul08/18_001279_Kim_Cattrall_And_Eva_Padberg_Help_Kick_Off_Mercedes_Benz_Fashion_Week_Berlin_Spring_Summer_2009.html |title=Kim Cattrall and Eva Padberg Help Kick Off Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week Berlin Spring/Summer 2009 &#124; eMercedesBenz - The Unofficial Mercedes-Benz Weblog |publisher=eMercedesBenz |date=2008-07-18 |accessdate=2009-10-17}}</ref>

The GLK-Class shares the [[Mercedes-Benz C-Class|C-Class]] (W204) platform, and production plans continued after the demise of the similar [[Smart (automobile)#formore|Smart Formore]].

==Safety==

Comprehensive restraint system for the best possible occupant protection.<ref>[http://www.emercedesbenz.com/Jul08/15_001261_The_Mercedes_Benz_GLK_Class_Active_And_Passive_Safety.html The Mercedes-Benz GLK-Class:  Active And Passive Safety - eMercedesBenz | The Unofficial Mercedes-Benz Weblog]</ref>

In combination with the front and rear deformation zones, the high-strength passenger cell of the GLK-Class provides a highly efficient foundation for the occupant protection systems. The intricate restraint system is capable of offering the greatest possible protection potential. As many as nine airbags are fitted, which are deployed selectively depending on the type of collision and its severity to reduce the forces exerted on the occupants [http://www.emercedesbenz.com eMercedesBenz] said - The all-encompassing approach taken to achieving a high standard of safety evinces itself particularly clearly in the Integral Safety concept, that Mercedes-Benz has once again implemented in the new GLK.

==Range==
At launch in Germany, the car was offered in [[all-wheel drive]] only, though recently a [[rear-wheel drive]] only version debuted.

The range at launch comprised:
* GLK 220 CDI 4MATIC - 2.2-Litre-I4 - {{convert|170|PS|kW hp|0|abbr=on}}
* GLK 280/300 (since 2009) 4MATIC - 3.0-Litre-V6 - {{convert|231|PS|kW hp|0|abbr=on}}
* GLK 350 4MATIC - 3.5-Litre-V6 - {{convert|272|PS|kW hp|0|abbr=on}}
* GLK 320 CDI/350 CDI (since 2009) 4MATIC - 3.0-Litre-Diesel - {{convert|224|PS|kW hp|0|abbr=on}}

Two new BlueEFFICIENCY models were added in September 2009
* GLK 220 CDI BlueEFFICIENCY - 2.2-Litre-I4 - {{convert|170|PS|kW hp|0|abbr=on}} (rear-wheel drive)
* GLK 250 CDI 4MATIC BlueEFFICIENCY - 2.2-Litre-I4 - {{convert|204|PS|kW hp|0|abbr=on}}
