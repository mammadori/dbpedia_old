The '''Mercedes-Benz Cito''' (coded as '''O520''') was a [[Low-floor bus|low-floor]] [[midibus]] built by [[Mercedes-Benz buses|Mercedes-Benz]] for [[Continental Europe]] between 1999 and 2003.

It was available in three lengths: 8 metre, 9 metre and 10 metre long.
