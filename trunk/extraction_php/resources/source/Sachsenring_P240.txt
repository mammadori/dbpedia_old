The '''Sachsenring P240''' was a [[luxury car]] built by the [[HQM Sachsenring GmbH|VEB Kraftfahrzeugwerk Horch Zwickau]] in [[East Germany]] between 1955 and 1959.  The early preproduction models can be identified by the grill, which is divided in two and has vertical bars.
