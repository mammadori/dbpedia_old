The '''Ruf RK Coupe/Spyder''' is a [[mid-engine design|mid-engined]] [[sports car]] made by [[Ruf Automobile]] and Studiotorino.

==Model Information==

A collaboration between [[Germany|German]] automaker Ruf and [[Italy|Italian]] [[coachbuilder]] Studiotorino, the Ruf RK Coupe/Spyder is based on the [[Porsche]] [[Porsche Cayman|Cayman]].  Available in both [[Coupe]] and [[Spyder]] formats, the RK takes design clues from the [[Ferrari Dino#Dino 246 GT & GTS|Ferrari Dino 246 GT]], the [[Porsche 550]], and the [[Porsche 904]].  With a [[flat-6]] 3.8 [[Litre|L]] [[supercharger|supercharged]] [[engine]] (the RK in Ruf Coupe/Spyder stands for ''Ruf Kompressor''), the RK Coupe pumps out 324 [[Kilowatt|kW]] (440 [[Horsepower|hp]]) at 7,000 [[Revolutions per minute|rpm]] and 470 [[Newton metre|N·m]] (347 [[Foot-pound force|ft·lbf]]) of [[torque]] at 5,500 rpm.

The RK Coupe and Spyder are both extremely limited production models; only a total of 49 of each was produced.

==Performance==

The RK Coupe, the better performer of the two (as the RK Spyder is a convertible), has a top speed of 314 [[Kilometres per hour|km/h]] (190 [[Miles per hour|mph]]), and [[acceleration|accelerates]] from 0-60mph in 3.8 [[second|s]].
