The '''Audi Avantissimo''' was a [[concept car]] by the [[Germany|German]] automobile manufacturer [[Audi]]. The Audi Avantissimo debuted at the 2001 [[Frankfurt Motor Show]] in Germany.  Audi planned the Avantissimo to be luxurious and distinguished, but very swift at the same time.  Many of its styling cues are visible on the 2004 [[Audi A8]].

The Audi Avantissimo had a 4.2&nbsp;L V8 engine equipped with twin [[turbochargers]].  This engine developed about {{Convert|430|PS|kW hp|0|abbr=on}} and {{Auto ft.lbf|600|-1}} of torque.  The Avantissimo had a six-speed [[automatic transmission]] and also had [[quattro (four wheel drive system)|quattro]] permanent [[four-wheel drive]].
