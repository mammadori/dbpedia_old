The '''Volkswagen Volksbus''' is a range of [[bus]] [[chassis]] built by [[Volkswagen Trucks and Buses]] since April 1993.

Today Volkswagen Trucks and Buses produce bus chassis in the 5 to 18&nbsp;[[tonne]] category, the majority of them are powered by [[MWM Motores Diesel Ltda|MWM]] engines.

==Conversions==
All bodies are assembled by private coachbuilders.  The coachbuilders then manufacture them to desired configuration e.g. school bus, shuttle bus, tour bus & coach buses etc.
