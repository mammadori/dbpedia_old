'''Rupinder Nagra''' is an actor who has been working Internationally. He has worked extensively in Canada and now finds his work takiing him to India and the UK. He was a nominee for Best Actor at the [[29th Genie Awards]] for his role in the film ''[[Amal (film)|Amal]]''.

He was raised in [[Hamilton, Ontario|Hamilton]], [[Ontario]].<ref>[http://www.blogto.com/film/2007/09/homegrown_talent_amals_rupinder_nagra/ Homegrown Talent: Amal's Rupinder Nagra]. ''blogTO'', September 10, 2007.</ref>
