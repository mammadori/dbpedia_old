The '''Porsche Roxster''' is a [[Compact SUV|compact]] [[Crossover (automobile)|crossover sport utility vehicle]] (SUV) proposed by [[Volkswagen]] chairman [[Martin Winterkorn]], to be released by German automaker [[Porsche]] for the 2010 or 2011 model year. It will be built with many components of the [[Audi Q5]] SUV.<ref>{{Cite journal | last = Kable | first =Greg  | title = This Little Porsche Went to Market | journal = [[AutoWeek]] | volume = 59 | issue = 22 | pages = 8-9 | publisher = [[Crain Communications Inc.]] | location = [[Detroit, Michigan]]   | date =November 2, 2009   | issn = 0192-9674}}</ref> As a compact SUV, it will be the second Porsche SUV, after the [[mid-size]] [[Porsche Cayenne]].
