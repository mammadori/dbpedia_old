The '''BMW F3''' is an upcoming [[compact SUV|compact]] [[Crossover (automobile)|crossover SUV]] to be released in the 2010 model year by German automaker [[BMW]].  It will be based on the [[BMW 3-Series]] platform.  It will slot below the [[BMW X3|X3]] in the lineup, and is expected to compete up against the [[Acura RDX]], [[Audi Q3]] and [[Mercedes-Benz GLK-Class]].
