The '''Porsche 787''' was a [[Formula 2]] racing car built in in [[1960]], based on the 1500 RS-K [[Racing|race]] [[sports car]]. 

Built to the 1960 Formula 2 [[Formula racing|formula]], the Type 787 was driven by such notables as [[Wolfgang von Trips]] and [[Dan Gurney]] to victory and obtaining the unofficial "Coupe des Constructeurs" â€” Formula 2's [[World Constructors Championship]].  The car had a [[flat-4]] [[engine]] developing 190 [[Horsepower|hp]] at 8,000 [[revolutions per minute|rpm]].

In [[1961 Formula One season|1961]], the 787 was used in [[Formula 1]] for a scant few races while the [[Porsche 718|718]] took part in most races.
