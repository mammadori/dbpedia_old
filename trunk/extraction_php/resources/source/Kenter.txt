The '''Kenter''' was a [[Germany|German]] [[automobile]] manufactured from 1923 until 1925.  Successor to the [[Komet (German automobile)|Komet]], it was available in either an sv four with 1060 cc [[Steudel]] [[engine]] or an [[Atos]]-engined model of 1305 cc.
