[[Image:1958 Porsche Diesel Junior F108.jpg|thumb|right|A 1958 Porsche-Diesel Junior F108.]]

'''Porsche Junior''' was a [[tractor]] from [[Porsche]] that was made between 1937 and 1960.

The Junior was powered by an air-cooled, 822 cc, single-cylinder [[diesel engine]] giving 14 [[horsepower|hp]].
