The '''Wenkelmobil''' was a [[Germany|German]] [[automobile]] manufactured from 1904 until 1907.  It was an early Wenkel design, with one- and two-cylinder [[proprietary engine]]s provided by [[De Dion]] and [[Fafnir (automobile)|Fafnir]], among others.
