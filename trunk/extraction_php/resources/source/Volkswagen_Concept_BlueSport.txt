The '''Volkswagen Concept BlueSport''' is a [[Mid-engine design|mid-engined]] [[roadster]] [[concept car]] produced by [[Volkswagen]].<ref>[http://www.autoblog.com/2009/01/11/detroit-2009-volkswagen-bluesport-concept-turbodiesel-done-rig/ Detroit 2009: Volkswagen BlueSport concept, turbodiesel done right]</ref><ref>[http://jalopnik.com/5128550/vw-bluesport-roadster-the-mid+engined-diesel-sportscar VW Bluesport Roadster: The Mid-Engined Diesel Sportscar]</ref> It was introduced at the 2009 [[North American International Auto Show]] in [[Detroit]]. The Concept BlueSport follows on from a previous roadster concept car, the [[Volkswagen Concept R]], shown at the 2003 [[Frankfurt Motor Show]].

The Concept BlueSport is powered by a {{convert|2.0|L|cuin|abbr=on}} [[TDI]] [[Straight-4|I4]] producing around {{convert|180|hp|kW|abbr=on}} and {{convert|260|lbft|Nm|abbr=on}}. A six-speed dual-clutch [[Direct shift gearbox|DSG gearbox]] helps give it an estimated 0-{{convert|60|mph|km/h|abbr=on}} time of 6.2&nbsp;sec and a top speed of {{convert|140|mph|km/h|abbr=on}}.

The vehicle is proposed to be built and assembled at Volkswagen's [[AutoEuropa]] plant in [[Palmela]], Portugal.
