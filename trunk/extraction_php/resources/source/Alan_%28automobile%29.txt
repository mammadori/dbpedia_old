The '''Alan''' was a [[Germany|German]] "inflation-period" [[automobile]] of simple design; it was manufactured by J Mayer in [[Bamberg]] in limited numbers between 1923 and 1925.  The only model, the 6/30, had a 30[[horsepower|hp]], four-[[cylinder (engine)|cylinder]] engine with overhead valves, made in [[Berlin]] by [[Siemens AG|Siemens and Halske]].
