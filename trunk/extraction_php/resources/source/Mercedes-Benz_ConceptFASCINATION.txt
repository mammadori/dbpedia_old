The '''Mercedes-Benz ConceptFASCINATION''' is a [[concept car]] created in [[Germany]] by [[Mercedes-Benz]]. It was revealed to the public on September 11 2008, and will appear at the [[2008 Paris Motor Show]]. The [[Shooting brake|shooting-brake]]-style concept has been designed as a preview for the next-generation ([[Mercedes-Benz W212|W212]]) [[Mercedes-Benz E-Class|Mercedes E-Class]], and will likely never make production.<ref name = CarMagazine>{{cite web | url = http://www.carmagazine.co.uk/News/Search-Results/First-Official-Pictures/Mercedes-Fascination-concept--the-new-E-class-coupe | title = Mercedes Fascination Concept | publisher = Car Magazine Online | accessdate = 2008-09-12 | }}</ref>

== Precursive Design Elements ==
In German car-design circles, the term 'Fascination' is used to describe a 'new direction', with various elements such as the nose and profile likely to appear on the next-generation E-Class, to be revealed at the 2009 [[Geneva motor show]], and its derivatives, including the [[Mercedes-Benz CLS|CLS]] 4-door [[coup√©]].<ref name = GoAuto>{{cite web | url = http://goauto.com.au/mellor/mellor.nsf/story2/680ED9E5BB4232A2CA2574C2000CDADA | title = Merc's E-Class Coupe Returns | publisher = GoAuto | accessdate = 2008-09-15 | }}</ref>

Mercedes-Benz director Dr Thomas Weber, responsible for [[research and development]] said,

<blockquote>
"The show car not only demonstrates a fascinating, new design idiom, but - with our new, high-performance and economical diesel drive system using BlueTEC technology - shows that Mercedes-Benz vehicles will also set standards in the future in terms of sustainability as well as emotional appeal, driving pleasure and comfort."<ref name = Zimbio>{{cite web | url = http://www.zimbio.com/Mercedes-Benz/articles/247/Mercedes+Benz+Concept+Fascination+Study | title = Mercedes-Benz Concept Fascination study | publisher = Zimbio | accessdate = September 15, 2008 | }}</ref>
</blockquote>

== Specifications ==
The ConceptFASCINATION is powered by a 2.2-litre four-cylinder [[BlueTec]] turbo-diesel engine, with [[Eco-innovation|emissions-cutting]] [[AdBlue]] technology. The engine develops 152[[KW#Kilowatt|kW]] of power and 500[[Newton metre|Nm]] of torque, and is combined with a 6-speed [[manual gearbox]]. This [[powertrain]] is likely to make production in various Mercedes-Benz models by 2010.<ref name=linform>{{cite web | url = http://loadinform.com/mercedes-fascination-concept | title = Mercedes Fascination Concept | publisher = Linform | accessdate = 2008-09-15 | }}</ref>

Detailed information regarding the ConceptFASCINATION will be revealed at the Paris motor show in October 2008.
