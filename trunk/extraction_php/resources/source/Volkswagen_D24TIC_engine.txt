The '''Volkswagen D24TIC''' is a 2.4&nbsp;[[litre]] [[straight-six engine|inline-6 cylinder]] [[overhead camshaft#single overhead camshaft|single overhead camshaft]] (SOHC) [[diesel engine]], formerly manufactured by [[Volkswagen Group]].

==Technical description & rated outputs==
The engine is constructed in an identical manner to the [[Volkswagen D24T engine]], and its earler [[Volkswagen D24 engine]] - but includes an [[intercooler]] to improve performance and consistency of performance.
