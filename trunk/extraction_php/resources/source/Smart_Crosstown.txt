The '''Smart Crosstown''' was a concept car by [[Smart (automobile)|Smart]] shown at the [[North American International Auto Show|Detroit Auto Show]], also known as NAIAS.  It was a gas/electric hybrid based on the [[Smart Fortwo]], currently being sold. It also featured a [[targa top]] style roof.<ref>http://www.automobilemag.com/auto_shows/frankfurt/0509_smart_crosstown_concept/index.html</ref>
