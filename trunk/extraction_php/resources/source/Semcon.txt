'''Semcon [[Aktiebolag|AB]]''' Semcon is a global company active in the areas of engineering services and product information. The Group has sites at 40 locations on three continents with around 2,800 employees who have extensive experience from many different development-intensive sectors. Semcon helps companies develop products that boost sales and competitive strength. This occurs in both single assignments and projects where specific teams are formed to develop innovative solutions for the entire product development chain from requirement studies to finished product<ref>[http://www.semcon.se/en-gb/menue/Financial/Investor-Relations-/Key-figures/ Semcon AB</ref>.

==History==

==Core businesses==
===Automotive R&D===
It is Semcon’s single biggest business area with customers such as Audi, BMW, Ford, GM, Mercedes Benz, Porsche, Scania, Saab, Volkswagen and AB Volvo.

===Design & Development===
Over 900 people work for this area. This encompasses varied sectors of activity like:
* Product Development (industrial design, mechanical design, production technology, polymer technology, prototypes, electricity and automation).
* Embedded Intelligent Solutions.
* Medical Life Science (pharmaceuticals/biotechnology and medicine technology).
* Semcon Project Management (training and service within project management).

===Informatic===
Semcon Informatic offers complete information solutions within customer service and interactive marketing, targeted for professional as well as consumer end-users.
