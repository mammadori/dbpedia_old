'''JE DESIGN GmbH''' is a [[Germany|German]] car [[engine tuning|tuning]] manufacturer.  They specialise in the four primary brands of [[Volkswagen Group]], namely [[Volkswagen]], [[Audi]], [[SEAT]], and [[Škoda Auto|Škoda]] cars.

JE DESIGN was founded by Jochen Eckelt in 1990.  They are based in the [[Leingarten]] municipality in the [[Heilbronn (district)|district of Heilbronn]], [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]].
