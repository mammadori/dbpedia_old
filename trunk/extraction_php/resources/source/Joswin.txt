The '''Joswin''' was a [[Germany|German]] [[automobile]] manufactured from 1920 until 1924.  Big luxury cars, they were powered by [[World War I|war]]-surplus [[Mercedes (car)|Mercedes]] six-cylinder, 12 [[spark plug]] [[aeroengine]]s of 6462&nbsp;cc and 7269&nbsp;cc which had been modified by designer [[Josef Winsch]]; construction took place at his works in [[Berlin-Halensee]].
