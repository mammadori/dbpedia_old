{{TOC right}}The '''Volkswagen Group PL71 platform''' is an [[automobile platform]] shared among various [[marque]]s of [[sport utility vehicle]]s (SUVs) ([[Four-wheel drive|4x4s]] in the [[Europe]]an market).  It was co-developed by [[Volkswagen Group]] and [[Porsche]] AG, and is used by Volkswagen Group for its [[Audi]] and [[Volkswagen|Volkswagen Passenger Cars]] marques.  All current platform users are built at [[Bratislava]] in [[Slovakia]].

==PL71 platform vehicles==
*[[Volkswagen Touareg]] (''Typ'' 7L)
*[[Porsche Cayenne]]
*[[Audi Q7]] (''Typ'' 4L)
