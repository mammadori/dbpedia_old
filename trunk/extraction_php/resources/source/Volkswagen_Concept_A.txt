The '''Volkswagen Concept A''' was a [[concept car]] created by [[Germany|German]] [[automobile]] manufacturer, [[Volkswagen]]. It was introduced at the 2006 [[Geneva Auto Show]]. The Concept A is a cross between a [[sportscar]] and an [[SUV]] which makes it rugged and fast.

==Description==
The Concept A is powered by a ''[[I4 engine|I4]] twincharger (TSI) engine'' which gives out a total of around 150 [[horsepower]].  It has [[all-wheel drive]] (AWD) with [[4MOTION]] and six gears with [[automatic transmission]].  The design of the car is very sporty, but still rugged.  It is sometimes claimed as a cross between a sportcar [[coupe]] and an SUV because of this styling and design.  A production version of the Concept A, the [[Volkswagen Tiguan|Tiguan]], is expected for sale in late 2008.
