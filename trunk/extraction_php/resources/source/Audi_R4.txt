The '''Audi R4''' is a small [[sports car]] expected to be built by [[Audi]].  The '''R4''' will have an electric variant as well as a gas-powered version.<ref name="Edmunds">{{cite web | title = Audi To Build R4 Electric Roadster | url = http://www.edmunds.com/insideline/do/News/articleId=159046 | publisher = Edmunds Inc. | date = October 14, 2009 | accessdate = October 19, 2009}}</ref>  Production versions are expected to enter the market in 2012.<ref name="Edmunds"/>  The vehicle will be the second Audi model to hold the 'R' moniker, after the [[Audi R8|R8]].  Like the R8, it will have a [[mid-engine]] configuration and will seat two.  It is expected to be available as a roadster.<ref name="Edmunds"/>

== Electric version ==
The electric version will be based on the e-Tron concept shown at the 2009 Frankfurt Auto Show.<ref name="Edmunds"/>  It will have a lithium-ion battery pack and four electric motors, one at each wheel.<ref name="Edmunds"/>
