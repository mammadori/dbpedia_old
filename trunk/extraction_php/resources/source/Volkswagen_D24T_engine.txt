The '''Volkswagen D24T engine''' is a 2.4&nbsp;[[litre]] [[straight-six engine|inline-6 cylinder]] [[overhead camshaft#single overhead camshaft|single overhead camshaft]] (SOHC) [[diesel engine]], formerly manufactured by [[Volkswagen Group]].

==Technical description==
The engine is constructed in an identical manner to the earler [[Volkswagen D24 engine]] - but includes an [[turbocharger]] to improve performance.
