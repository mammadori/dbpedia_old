The '''Mercedes-Benz E550''' is the flagship [[E-class]] sedan (the [[Mercedes E63 AMG]] is the super sedan for this class, as the [[S63 AMG]] and [[S65 AMG]] are for the [[S-Class]]), that offers affordability (for a company like Mercedes) and power for a sedan.

==Performance==
===Specs===
*Engine: 382&nbsp;hp 5.5L
*Transmission(s): 5-speed [[automatic transmission|automatic]]<br>7-speed [[automatic transmission|automatic]]<br>6-speed [[Manual transmission|manual]]
*Drivetrain: RWD (Rear Wheel Drive)
*Top Speed: 155&nbsp;mph (electronically limited)
*0-60&nbsp;mph: 4.9 seconds
*MSRP: $53,000

===Performance inforamtion===
The E550 falls into the category of "super sedans", which includes sedans like the [[Cadillac CTS-V]], [[BMW M5]], and [[Mercedes E63 AMG]]. The E550 is a virtual carbon-copy of the [[E63 AMG]], with the main difference being that the E550 is slightly less powerful.{{Citation needed|date=October 2009}} With the limiter off, the E550 can reach a top speed of around 195&nbsp;mph, and accelerate from 0-60&nbsp;mph in under 5 seconds. The E550 Retirns [[United States Environmental Protection Agency‎|U.S. Environmental Protection Agency]] fuel economy estimates of 18mpg (city), and 26mpg (highway), as well as combined fuel economy of 21mpg. 

====Suspension====
In the front, the E550 features a 4-link suspension, and 5-link, multi-arm suspension in the rear.

====Brakes====
The E550's brakes are 4-wheel, ABS disc brakes that are 12.7in×1.3in. in the front, and 11.8×0.9in. in the rear.
====Tires====
The E550's 17in. rims are shod with Pirelli 245/45R17 tires in the front and rear.

====Standard luxury features====
The E550 as with all of Mercedes' vehicles comes with a long list of standard luxury features. 
*MB Tex seat trim
*10 way power front bucket seats with 3 position memory, adjustable lumbar support, and power active head restraints.
*Front/rear floor mats
*4 way power tilt/telescopic steering column with programmable easy-exit feature
*Leather wrapped steering wheel with multi function controls.
*Quartz clock.
*Electronic fuel bar graph & coolant temp.
*Cargo area and cabin 12V power outlets.
*Electronically heated rear window with auto shut off feature.
*Anti-theft system with engine immobilizer.
*Burl-Walnut trim.
*Auto dimming rear view mirror.

===Exterior===
====Exterior features====
*17in. multi-spoke aluminum wheels.
*Power tilting/sliding sunroof.
*Variable-focus halogen headlamps with projector low-beams.
*Power heated  mirrors with memory, and auto dimming.
*Rain sensing windshield wipers with heated washer fluid tank.
*Light sensing headlamps.

The E550 will be featured in a sedan, sport wagon (N/A in the US), and coupe body styles.
