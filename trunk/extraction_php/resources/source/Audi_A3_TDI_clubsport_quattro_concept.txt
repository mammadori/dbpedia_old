The '''Audi A3 TDI clubsport quattro''' is a [[concept car]] unveiled in 2008.  Based on the [[Audi A3]], it is an approach by [[Audi AG|Audi]] to address both performance and the environment.

==Drivetrain and chassis==
The engine in the concept car is a turbocharged 2.0 liter diesel producing {{convert|224|hp}} and {{convert|332|ft.lbf|N.m|abbr=on}}. of torque.  It  produces {{convert|113.8|hp}} per liter while meeting Euro 5 Diesel emission standards.  It uses the [[quattro (four wheel drive system)|quattro]] drive system with a six-speed manual transmission.

The chassis has Audi's Magnetic Ride Suspension system, lowering the vehicle {{convert|1.4|in|mm}} from the base model.  It also has ceramic front brakes, a four-link rear suspension and electro-mechanical servo assist for the [[rack and pinion]] steering.<ref name = qq>{{cite journal | last =Rivera| first =Paul| title =Audi A3 TDI clubsport quattro concept| journal =quattro quarterly | volume = XVIII | issue = 3 | pages = 43â€“44 | publisher = Audi Club North America | date = Summer 2008}}</ref>

==Exterior and interior==
Exterior modifications include the widening of the grill, modifying the odd, and enlarging air intrakes.  Bolt-on fender flares and a large rear spoiler have been added.  The interior changes include sport seats and a flat-bottomed steering wheel.<ref name = qq/>

==Performance==
Audi claims performance of 0 to {{convert|62|mph|km/h}} in 6.6 seconds with a top speed of {{convert|149|mph|km/h}}.<ref>{{Citation | last =Monticello | first =Mike | author-link = | last2 = | first2 = | author2-link = | title =Audi A3 TDI Clubsport Quattro Concept | journal =[[Road & Track]] | volume = | issue = | pages =20 | date =August 2008 | url =http://www.roadandtrack.com/article.asp?section_id=10&article_id=6848 | doi = | id = }}</ref>  It is expected to get approximately {{convert|40|mpgus|L/100 km mpgimp}}.
