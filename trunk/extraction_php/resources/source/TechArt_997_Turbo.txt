The '''TechArt 997 Turbo''', also known as simply the '''TechArt Turbo''', is a high-performance [[sports car]] based on the [[Porsche 997]] Turbo.  [[TECHART|TechArt]] has modified the car extensively, fitting it with a body kit, interior modifications, and significant engine tuning.

==Design and Features==
To the stock [[Porsche 997]] Turbo, TechArt has added a body kit featuring new front and rear [[spoiler]]s, [[carbon fibre]] grille, headlight covers, and side skirts.  The car is fitted with TechArt's own 20" wheels, with [[Michelin]] Pilot tires to improve handling.  The interior is trimmed out with carbon fiber and custom upholstery as well.  The most significant modification to the car is under the engine lid.  TechArt has modified the stock {{convert|3.6|L|cuin|1}} [[flat-6]] with larger [[turbocharger]]s, improved [[engine control unit]] chip, and stainless steel exhaust system.  With these modifications, the engine develops {{convert|600|hp|kW|0}} at 6400 [[rpm]] and {{Auto ft.lbf|609|0}} of [[torque]] at 3700 rpm, allowing for a top speed of {{convert|210|mph|km/h|1|abbr=on}}.  Customers pay for the base [[Porsche 997|997 Turbo]], which costs $122,900 [[USD]], then add the desired modifications; the bodykit costs $11,000 [[USD]], while the engine upgrades are $29,500 [[USD]].  The full package retails for about $186,000 [[USD]].
