The '''Excelsior-Mascot''' was a [[Germany|German]] [[automobile]] manufactured in [[Cologne-Nippes]] from 1911 until 1922.  Only a few, with two- and four-cylinder proprietary [[engine]]s of 8&nbsp;hp to 18&nbsp;hp, were produced.
